Christmas at Palm Springs, CA

For the week leading up to Christmas, my wife's parents rented an Airbnb in Palm Springs, CA. It's about 2 hours from Los Angeles, where my brother-in-law is currently attending medical school, and about 2 hours from San Diego.

The desert climate at Palm Springs makes the days warmer and the evenings cooler. The mountain state parks nearby also allows you to see winter climate due to the higher altitudes.
